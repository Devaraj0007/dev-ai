Northwind Analytics — Employee Handbook (2026 Edition)

1. Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week without prior manager approval. Fully remote arrangements require written approval from both the employee's manager and the People Operations team, and are reviewed every six months. Employees working remotely must be reachable during core hours, which are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM in their local time zone.

2. Leave Policy
Full-time employees accrue 18 days of paid time off (PTO) per calendar year, accrued monthly at a rate of 1.5 days per month. Unused PTO can be carried over into the following year up to a maximum of 10 days; any balance beyond that is forfeited on December 31. New hires begin accruing PTO from their start date and are not eligible to take PTO during their first 30 days of employment, except in the case of documented medical emergencies.

3. Parental Leave
Northwind Analytics offers 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave to any employee who has completed at least 12 months of continuous service, regardless of gender or the nature of the parenting event (birth, adoption, or fostering). Employees with less than 12 months of service are eligible for 6 weeks of paid leave. Parental leave can be taken in a single continuous block or split into two blocks within the first year after the qualifying event.

4. Expense Reimbursement
Employees must submit expense reports within 30 days of incurring the expense. Reports submitted after 30 days require director-level approval. All reimbursements over $500 require two levels of approval: the direct manager and a finance business partner. Reimbursements are typically processed within 10 business days of approval.

5. Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year, in June and December. Ratings use a five-point scale from "Needs Improvement" to "Outstanding." Compensation adjustments tied to performance reviews take effect in the pay cycle immediately following the review, typically within 45 days.

6. Equipment Policy
New employees receive a standard equipment package consisting of a laptop, a monitor, and a headset. Employees may request additional equipment through the IT portal, subject to manager approval. All equipment remains company property and must be returned within 14 days of an employee's last working day.
